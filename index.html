About me

I'm a research mathematician with a background in programming and computer science. I'm interested in transitioning to a career in machine learning with a research component.

Mathematics research

My mathematics research studies an intersection between dynamical systems, topology, and differential geometry (at a basic level, the tools I rely on boil down to analysis and linear algebra). Recently, I have been studying connections between Gelfand-Zeitlin systems and the exciting field of tropical geometry. You can read more about it on my University of Geneva department webpage, or in this post.
